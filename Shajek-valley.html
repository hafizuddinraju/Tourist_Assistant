Description

There will be arrangement of various amusements for making your vacation more enjoyable and memorable. Hope we will provide you a memorable trip by arranging this event.

Tour Itinerary:


Day-1:

Our journey will start from Dhaka to Khagrachari at 8:00pm by Bus. This Bus is under the rut of Dhaka to Khagrachari. It is very comfortable journey for everyone specially those are patient (Overnight Journey).

Day-2:

In early morning we will arrive at Khagrachari. After breakfast - start journey for Sajek valley by Bus which will take 3 hours. After arrive at sajek valley check into the Resort and take lunch at Restaurant within 1.30 pm. After lunch go to the famous Place. We wil take rest and Dinner time at 9.00 Pm.

Day-3:

Early in the morning we will enjoy beautiful Weather. We will take breakfast within 7.00 to 8.00 Am. B.B.Q Dinner at 8.00 Pm. After completing our dinner we are ready for Cultural Program

Day-4:

We will take breakfast within 7.30 to 8.00 Am. Then you will get free time for Shopping.Lunch time 1.30 Pm. After taking lunch we will get ready with luggage to ride on the Bus for comeback to Khagrachari . Then In the Night Khagrachari to Dhaka .Launch start journey at 9.00 Pm. .

Day-5:

Early morning we will reach Dhaka. End of the tour.


Package Included :

> Accommodation with Standard category Non AC hotel share basis
> Dhaka to Khagrachari By Bus
> Khagrachari to Sajek valley By Chader Gari
> Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
> Guide service
